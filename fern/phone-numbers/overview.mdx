---
title: "Phone Numbers"
description: "Acquiring and configuring Ortavox Telephony"
---
Ortavox serves as your complete SIP and telephony provider.

You can instantly provision local, mobile, or toll-free numbers from inside the Ortavox dashboard or programmatically via the API. Once acquired, attach an Agent to the number, and it will pick up immediately when dialed.

## Bring Your Own Carrier (BYOC)
If you already possess a robust telephony infrastructure, you can SIP-Trunk directly into Ortavox's media servers to maintain your carrier relationships.
