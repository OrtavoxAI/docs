# Architecture

Ortavox is built to seamlessly orchestrate high-throughput, ultra-low latency audio streaming between your end clients and upstream AI models.

## Components

### Frontend Clients
Your end-users can connect to Ortavox from multiple interfaces:
- **Mobile & Browser**: Using the Ortavox Client SDKs via WebRTC.
- **Telephony**: Standard SIP trunking for inbound and outbound calls.

### Ortavox Core
The engine that powers it all. It handles secure websocket/WebRTC connections, Media Handling, LLM orchestration, and your custom Business Logic (Tools).

### AI Providers
Ortavox proxies audio and text securely to leading AI providers (OpenAI, Deepgram, ElevenLabs, etc) while maintaining persistent websocket connections to minimize latency.
