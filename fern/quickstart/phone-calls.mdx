description: "Creating and receiving real-time phone calls (PSTN) with your AI agents"
---
You can instantly deploy Ortavox AI Assistants onto real telephone numbers across the globe.

We manage all underlying telecommunication routing, SIP bridges, and Carrier integrations so you can simply assign a phone number from your Dashboard and begin receiving (or dispatching) calls immediately.

## Overview
- **Inbound Calls**: A user dials your Ortavox Number.
- **Outbound Calls**: You ask the API to dial a user's number.
- **Transfers**: You programmatically transfer an ongoing call to a human specialist.
